Baking on a Budget

Welcome to Baking on a Budget! Why use our recipes? Visitors of this site will be able to find not only delicious recipes, but also the prices of these recipes to fit their budget. Recipes on this site include baked feta pasta, chicken caeser wraps, and breakfast burritos. This website is for anyone, whether you struggle with making meal plans like me, and want an easy meal while keeping a tight budget or you want something fun and delicious to bake without breaking the bank!
